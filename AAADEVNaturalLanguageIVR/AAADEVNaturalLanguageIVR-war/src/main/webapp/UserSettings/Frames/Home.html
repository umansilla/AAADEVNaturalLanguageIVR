Guide Home Section

The goal of the Home section is mainly to provide quick access to the available demos.

The title of the available demos is shown in red, to go directly to the corresponding demo site it is necessary to click on the blue button with the phrase "Go"

Guide BioMetrics Section

In the BioMetrics section, it allows the user to:

  • Create a user in Verbio BioMetrics
  • Record voice from the browser and listen to the result at the moment.
  • Analyze the result of an audio recorded at the moment
  • Validate the audio files that have been uploaded in Verbio BioMetrics
